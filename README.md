MoneyMap is a simple and efficient web app for tracking income and expenses. It features a clean, minimal UI similar to the one on your account, making it easy to use and visually appealing. Users can quickly add, edit, and manage transactions, categorize their spending, and view real-time updates of their total balance.

The app provides a clear overview of income and expenses through an organized dashboard, helping users understand their financial habits better. Basic charts offer quick insights into spending patterns, making it easier to identify areas to save.

With smooth navigation and responsive design, MoneyMap works seamlessly across devices. It focuses on simplicity and usability, making personal finance management straightforward, organized, and effective for everyday use.
